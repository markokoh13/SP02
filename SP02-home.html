Membership

Joining our club is easy! Membership benefits include:

  • Access to exclusive events and workshops
  • Opportunities to perform and collaborate
  • Discounts on music-related products and services
  • A supportive community of fellow music lovers

Membership is open to all students at Tarrant County College. To join, simply fill out our online application form and attend one of our meetings.
